About Us

Founded in 2006, Agile Richmond is the premier community of Agile practitioners in the Central Virginia region.

Anchored by our annual conference, InnoVAte Virginia, we connect local Agilists with their peers in the community, while supporting the adoption, use, and growth of Agile practices in the businesses and organizations in the Greater Richmond area.

Supporting Local Businesses

Agile practices can be found in companies of all sizes throughout the Greater Richmond area, and Agile Richmond supports the community in continuing to learn together.

Supporting Local Agilists

Our Monthly Meetings are a great place for our community of Agile practitioners and growing experts to share their knowledge and work on their public speaking skills.

Supporting Our Larger Community

Agile Richmond is an active part of our community, working with other local organizations to encourage other user groups and create a path toward speaking to a regional, national, and global audience.
